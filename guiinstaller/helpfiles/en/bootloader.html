Bootloader

Here you can customize options of your bootloader. You can simply press "Next" to apply defaults, which better in common case, or you can modify some settings.

Basic settings

Here you can choose place to install bootloader: bootable hard disk or bootable partition. You must remember that if you choosing installing into partition you must provide another bootloader in MBR!

Advanced settings

Here you can enable:

  • Placing /tmp in RAM: you can place /tmp in RAM for speed-upping your system. You must have at least 2GiB of RAM, in other case, you can feel slowless, not faster system.
  • Enable boot delay: you can enable pause in booting, if you have slow SSD of HDD. Nowadays it's deprecated, because initrd-script should do it automatically if needs.

Также вы можете задать дополнительные опции для ядра и включить дополнительные модули в initrd.
