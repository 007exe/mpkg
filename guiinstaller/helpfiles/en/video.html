Choosing a driver

By default, xorg detects a driver automatically. If your video card fails to work with this, choose a specific driver instead. Good fallback variants are fbdev, modesetting and vesa.

ATM, Intel GMA500/600/3600/3650 and other PowerVR-based cards are known to work only with fbdev or modesetting drivers, specified manually.

Special note for NVIDIA users

If you wish, you can choose a proprietary driver for your card. If you prefer this, choose a driver corresponding to your video card's model: new driver won't work with old cards.
